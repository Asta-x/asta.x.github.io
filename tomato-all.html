Agridera Fresh Hot Peppers

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of hot pepper seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world.

Agridera Hot Pepper breeding programs focus on three main segments: Cayenne-type peppers, Habanero-type peppers, and Ornamental hot peppers.
Cayenne-type Peppers

Mediterranean and Asian markets. Long thin fruits 10–15 cm. Pungency 30 000–50 000 SHU. Resistant to PVY and CMV.

Habanero-type Peppers

Caribbean and Latin American markets. Wrinkled fruits with fruity aroma. Extreme pungency 100 000–350 000 SHU.

Ornamental Hot Peppers

European retail markets. Compact plants with colorful cluster fruits. Suitable for pot cultivation.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
