Agridera Fresh Hot Peppers

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of hot pepper seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining resistances to major pepper viruses (PVY, CMV, TMV) with superior fruit quality traits. Our breeding programs focus on Scoville heat units, fruit color, shelf life, and disease tolerance.

Agridera Hot Pepper breeding programs focus on three main segments: Cayenne-type peppers, Habanero-type peppers, and Ornamental hot peppers.
Cayenne-type Peppers

Bred for Mediterranean and Asian markets. Long thin fruits, 10–15 cm, used dried or fresh. High pungency (30 000–50 000 SHU). Resistance to PVY and CMV.

Habanero-type Peppers

Bred for Caribbean and Latin American markets. Wrinkled lantern-shaped fruits with fruity aroma. Extreme pungency (100 000–350 000 SHU). Drought tolerant.

Ornamental Hot Peppers

Bred for European retail markets. Compact plants with colorful cluster fruits. Suitable for pot cultivation. Medium pungency (10 000–40 000 SHU).

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
